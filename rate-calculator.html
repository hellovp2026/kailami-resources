Quick reality check on rates

Beginner range (0–6 months): $5–$10/hr is realistic on Upwork to build your first reviews. On OnlineJobs.ph, expect PHP 25,000–40,000/month for full-time work.

1–2 years experience: $10–$20/hr is fair. You should have 3–5 client reviews and a clear niche by now.

2+ years specialized: $20–$40/hr is achievable for niche work (technical VAs, marketing strategists, executive assistants for founders). You're not just executing tasks — you're solving problems.

If your calculator says you need $60/hr to hit your goal but you have 0 reviews, that's a sign to either lower your goal, increase your hours, or be patient and build your way up.
