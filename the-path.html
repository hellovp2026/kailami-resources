There are over 30 free tools, templates, and guides on this site. Most VAs get overwhelmed and quit on day three because they don't know what to do first. This page solves that.

Click "Start" below. I'll walk you through everything — niche, certs, portfolio, applications, discovery calls, onboarding — in the right order, on a realistic schedule. You just show up every day and do the work.

Read this before you click start

You only become a VA if you deserve to.

Let me be honest with you, because nobody else will: becoming a VA is not a side hustle, a get-rich-quick gig, or something you do when you're bored. It's real, professional work that takes 2-3 months of consistent effort before you see your first paycheck. And then it's daily work to keep the clients coming.

The VAs who succeed aren't smarter than you, more talented than you, or luckier than you. They just put in the fucking work. Every single day. While you were waiting for "the right time" to start, they were on day 12 of applications. While you were "still researching niches," they had already landed their first client.

Here's who should close this tab right now:

  • Anyone looking for a "VA hack" or shortcut to skip the work
  • Anyone who thinks they'll do this "when they have time" — you won't
  • Anyone unwilling to commit at least 2 hours a day for 30 days
  • Anyone who quits the moment something gets uncomfortable
  • Anyone who needs to be motivated by someone else to keep going

If any of that is you, this page isn't for you. Close it. Save your time. Go look for a 9-to-5.

If you're still reading — good. You're the kind of person this works for. Now let's go.
